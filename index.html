Who We Are

A Legacy of
Precision

E.R. Automotives is your trusted partner in precision engineering and manufacturing for the automotive and general allied industries. With decades of excellence, we have established ourselves as a premier provider of high-quality precision machined, turned, and forging components.

Our commitment to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction has driven us to the forefront of the industry — delivering exceptional solutions that power vehicles and industries worldwide from our facility in Rohtak, Haryana.
